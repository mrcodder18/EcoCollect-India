Strict Segregation Guidelines

Proper segregation at the source is the key to recycling. Please follow these guidelines before placing your bins out for collection.

🟢 Wet Waste

Geela Kachra / Compostable

Accepted:

  • Vegetable & fruit peels
  • Leftover food, meat bones & eggshells
  • Tea leaves & coffee grounds
  • Pooja flowers & garden leaves
  • Soiled paper napkins / tissues

Strictly NOT Accepted:

  • Plastic wrappers & biscuit packets
  • Rubber bands (often found in food delivery parcels)
  • Diapers, sanitary pads (Bio-medical)
  • Pet litter / animal waste

🔵 Dry Waste

Sukha Kachra / Recyclables

Accepted:

  • Clean plastic bottles & food containers (washed)
  • Cardboard boxes, newspaper (Raddi)
  • Washed milk packets (please cut open, do not snip corners)
  • Glass bottles, metal cans & foil

Strictly NOT Accepted:

  • Food-soiled paper (e.g., greasy pizza boxes must be torn apart)
  • Broken glass or sharp objects (Wrap securely in paper and mark "SHARP")
  • Styrofoam (Thermocol)

🔴 E-Waste

E-Kachra / Specialized

Accepted:

  • Old laptops, mobile phones & keyboards
  • Tangled wires, chargers & earphones
  • Dead batteries (AA, AAA, Li-ion)
  • Old routers, switches & power banks

Strictly NOT Accepted:

  • Broken tubelights / CFL bulbs (Hazardous: contains Mercury)
  • Industrial chemicals or wet paint cans
  • Medical waste (syringes, expired medicines)
